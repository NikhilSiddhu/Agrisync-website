The AgriSync v3.0 Pipeline

From soil to satellite to screen: a 6-layer intelligence architecture for 97%+ accuracy.

sensors 01 / Sensing

Multi-Modal Field Node

7-in-1 RS485 soil sensor measuring NPK, pH, EC, moisture, and soil temperature. BME680 atmospheric sensor for temp, humidity, pressure, and VOC. Leaf wetness sensor, tipping bucket rain gauge, MEMS acoustic microphone for pest detection, and ESP32-CAM for visual leaf inspection. All from a single node at the field centroid.

settings_input_antenna 02 / Transmission

Jio 5G via SIM7600G

Direct cellular uplink with no LoRa gateway and no WiFi dependency. SIM7600G module transmits MQTT JSON payloads over Jio 5G directly to Azure IoT Hub. ESP32 buffers readings locally during any connectivity gaps.

satellite 03 / Satellite Fusion

Multi-Source Data Assimilation

Sentinel-2 NDVI and crop health at 10m every 5 days. Sentinel-1 SAR soil moisture through cloud cover at 10m every 6 days. NASA POWER API for solar radiation and ET0. MODIS for land surface temperature. IMD for live rainfall and district forecast. All fused with ground sensor data via Extended Kalman Filter.

grid_on 04 / Field Digital Twin

10m × 10m Spatial Intelligence

SRTM DEM topographic model divides the field into a 10m grid. Slope, drainage accumulation, and aspect assign a static risk profile to every cell. Kriging Gaussian Process interpolation extends the single node reading across the entire field. One sensor becomes a virtual sensor grid.

psychology 05 / Physics + AI Ensemble

Confidence-Gated ML Stack

DSSAT-lite phenology model tracks cabbage growth stage daily. Temporal Transformer TiDE forecasts 72 hours ahead. Crop-stage-aware XGBoost classifies disease risk per zone. MobileNetV2 CNN detects leaf disease from camera images. INMP441 acoustic classifier detects pests on the ESP32 chip itself. All models vote through a confidence-gated ensemble — alert fires only when 2 or more signals agree AND model historical accuracy exceeds 85%.

notifications_active 06 / Output

WhatsApp + Dashboard + SMS

WhatsApp Business API sends rich alerts with ESP32-CAM zone images attached. Farmer replies Y/N or sends a photo for AI re-verification, feeding directly into monthly model retraining. Public dashboard shows per-zone field heat map and 72-hour forecast. Fast2SMS as plain SMS fallback for non-WhatsApp farmers.

Model 01

Temporal Transformer (TiDE)

72-hour weather and soil time-series forecasting. Retains full-season patterns across the 90-120 day cabbage growing cycle. Replaces LSTM.

Model 02

XGBoost Stage-Aware

Disease risk classification per micro-zone. Activates disease rules only at the correct crop growth stage from the DSSAT-lite phenology model.

Model 03

MobileNetV2 CNN

Leaf disease detection from ESP32-CAM images. Pre-trained on 54,000+ PlantVillage Brassica disease photos. Runs on Pi 5.

Model 04

Acoustic Classifier

Runs on ESP32 chip directly via TF Lite Micro. Detects Cabbage Looper and Diamondback Moth feeding frequencies weeks before visible damage.

Model 05

Kriging / Gaussian Process

Spatial uncertainty mapping across the entire field grid from one sensor anchor point using SRTM drainage structure.

Model 06

DSSAT-lite Phenology

Tracks cabbage growth stage day-by-day from transplant date. Context layer that makes all other models crop-stage aware.

Crop Intelligence

Built for Banda Gobi

Cabbage-specific disease intelligence from ICAR-IIVR Varanasi and NHB research

AgriSync v3.0 is the first version optimised for a specific crop. Ten disease and stress conditions are encoded as rule-based synthetic training data, giving the AI disease knowledge from Day 1.

Peronospora parasitica

Downy Mildew

Night temp 8-16°C + RH above 85% + leaf wet + fog. 48-hour alert window.

Xanthomonas campestris

Black Rot (Xcc)

Warm humid conditions + rain splash + temp above 25°C. 24-48 hour alert window.

Rhizoctonia / Pythium

Damping Off

Cool cloudy + RH above 90% + wet compact soil. Seedling stage only.

Physiological Stress

Tipburn

Temp spike above 28°C + RH below 50% + rapid head fill phase. 12-hour window.

DBM & Cabbage Looper

Pest Alert

Acoustic feeding signal + NDVI edge degradation. Immediate alert, weeks before visible damage.
