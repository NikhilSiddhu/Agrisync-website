What is AgriSync

A digital agronomist that reads your field from space and field.

AgriSync watches one farmer's field with free satellite, sensors and weather data, learns what's normal for it, and sends warning before trouble shows.

Disease, days earlyFlagged 3–7 days before a spot reaches a leaf.
Water, on timeA 72-hour soil-moisture outlook for every irrigation.
Growth, in contextAdvice matched to the crop's real stage, not a calendar.

One missed disease can cost a season. AgriSync turns data overhead into decisions on the ground.

How it works

One Raspberry Pi runs the whole pipeline.

Each night, one device pulls free data, runs the models and messages every farmer by sunrise.

01

Satellites

Sentinel-2 tracks crop health; Sentinel-1 radar reads soil moisture through clouds. Both free, no field hardware.

02

Weather

Daily temperature, rainfall and humidity from NASA POWER, with forecasts from Open-Meteo — pulled each morning.

03

One Pi at the edge

A single Raspberry Pi 5 in the village does it all — fetch data, run models, gets data from farm sensors, send messages.

04

Daily pipeline

Scheduled jobs run through the night and finish the full sequence before 6:00 AM IST — every day.

05

Local data

Records stay on-device in SQLite and DuckDB. Nothing leaves the Pi by default — the farmer's data stays put.

06

Message back

Farmers get a short advisory, and can reply with a photo for an instant on-device diagnosis.

Intelligence

The PhytoPlex model stack.

Four focused models, each doing one job well — soil, growth, disease and a photo diagnosis when the farmer asks.

Model A · Soil moisture

XGBoost regressor

Predicts soil wetness now and 72 hours ahead from radar, rainfall and crop water loss.

Model B · Crop stage

Growing-degree days

Tracks cabbage from transplant to harvest by summing daily heat — simple, accurate within days.

Model C · Disease risk

Rules + matched filter + BOCPD

Three signals catch disease early: weather rules, a satellite-pattern match, and a field-anomaly alert.

Model D · Photo diagnosis

Moondream 2 vision

Send a WhatsApp photo; an on-device vision model names the symptom and suggests treatment.

Why it matters

Built from a village, designed for millions.

Hyper-local

Every rule, disease window and message is tuned to one real cabbage field in Harpur, UP — not a generic crop.

Scales by cluster

One Pi serves 20–50 fields. The next village just needs another Pi — nothing new in any farmer's field.

Free & open

Satellite, weather and the vision model are free or open-source. Farmers pay nothing.
